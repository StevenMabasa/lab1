About Me

Latest Project

I am currently working on a project for 52nd Holding, an insurance brokerage company based in Randburg, alongside a team of 20 other students. The company requires an application to manage its database, display a list of customers and their registered policies, and upload its services and insurance policies. Customers should be able to sign up for an insurance policy through the application, view their registered policies, and have the option to cancel or upgrade a policy without needing to call or visit the company's headquarters in person.
